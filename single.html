What we're looking for

Discover the qualities we seek in the next generation of modeling stars.

At Supreme Management, we are always on the lookout for fresh, unique, and inspiring talent to join our roster of extraordinary models. Whether you’re dreaming of walking runways, starring in campaigns, or making your mark in editorial, we’re here to help shape your journey. Here’s what we’re looking for:

1.Unique and Authentic Individuals

We celebrate diversity in all its forms—whether it’s your look, personality, or story. Confidence, character, and authenticity are just as important as physical appearance.

2.Professional Potential

Age: We scout talent as young as 14 (with parental consent for minors). There’s no “perfect” age to start—every journey is unique.

Height: Typically, models for high-fashion runways are 5’8” (173 cm) or taller, but commercial and niche markets may have different requirements.

Physique: A healthy, well-maintained physique suited to your chosen modeling category (e.g., runway, commercial, fitness, or plus-size).

3.Natural Beauty & adaptability

We look for natural features that stand out and can adapt to various fashion styles and roles.

Strong facial expressions and the ability to move confidently in front of the camera are key.

4.Ambition and drive

Modeling is a competitive industry. We seek individuals who are passionate, hardworking, and ready to take on challenges.

A positive attitude and willingness to learn go a long way in setting you apart.

5.Global and Local Talent

Whether you’re based in a major city or a small town, we believe talent can come from anywhere.

We have the tools and expertise to guide you from discovery to international success.

Ready to take the first step?

If you believe you have what it takes, we’d love to hear from you! Get in touch with Lea on Instagram or Tiktok, also feel free to apply on our official site. Remember, the best version of you is the one we want to see—be yourself and let your personality shine!
