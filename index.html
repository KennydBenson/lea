The Amazing Mrs wagner

A proud model scout for Supreme Management and a mother of two wonderful boys.

November 1, 2022

Her journey in the modeling industry began over a decade ago as a model with Supreme Management, where she had the privilege of walking runways, gracing editorials, and collaborating with some of the most creative minds in fashion. After 8 years of living her dream as a model, She discovered a new passion: helping others achieve theirs. Transitioning into scouting allowed her to leverage her industry experience and keen eye for talent to identify and nurture the next generation of modeling stars. At Supreme Management, we believe in more than just beauty; we value individuality, authenticity, and the unique stories that each model brings. As someone who’s been on both sides of the industry, She understands the challenges and opportunities that aspiring models face. Her goal is to guide you through the process, from discovery to success, with the care and insight of someone who’s been in your shoes.

Our Philosophy

Founded in 2000, Supreme Management New York has forged a path as a leader in redefining beauty, individuality, and the power of true representation. From our inception, we have been dedicated to challenging and transforming the fashion industry, showcasing talent that defies the conventional and embodies an uncompromising authenticity. Our models are not only striking in appearance but are also storytellers, voices, and icons of resilience and character. At Supreme, inclusion isn’t a goal—it’s our foundation. We are committed to elevating a new standard of excellence where unique identities and varied perspectives are celebrated without limits. Every career we build is a partnership rooted in respect, vision, and cultural impact. Supreme is more than an agency; we are a collective that pushes forward with purpose, inspiring the industry and the world to see beauty as boundless, inclusive, and reflective of real-life experiences. Together, we stand at the forefront of an evolving world where individuality leads and legacy is shaped anew every day.
